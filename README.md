# Business Problem

As organisations scale digitally, they face recurring challenges:

* Knowledge trapped in silos despite advanced digital tools
* Difficulty converting individual expertise into organisational capability
* Slow learning cycles in fast-moving markets
* Weak linkage between KM initiatives and business outcomes

Key questions addressed:

* How do leading organisations design KM systems that actually scale?
* How does KM enable (or limit) digital transformation?
* What role do platforms, people, and culture play in knowledge flow?
* How can KM create sustained competitive advantage, not just documentation?
